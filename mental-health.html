Outpatient Mental Health Care

A Full Mental Health Track — Not Just Addiction Care

Golden State Rehab is known for addiction treatment, but mental health is at the heart of everything we do. Our outpatient mental health program in Los Angeles treats the full spectrum of conditions — from depression and anxiety to trauma, PTSD, bipolar disorder, and co-occurring substance use — with the same evidence-based, compassionate care.

Whether you need the structure of a day program or the flexibility of evening sessions, our licensed clinicians build a plan around your life. You don't have to step away from work, school, or family to get real help.

Levels of Care

Mental Health Programs That Fit Your Life

From the most structured to the most flexible — all outpatient, all in Los Angeles.

Mental Health PHP

Our most structured option — full clinical days, five days a week. Ideal for stabilizing acute symptoms.

Mental Health IOP

Intensive outpatient care a few days a week — strong support while you keep working or studying.

Evening IOP

Evening mental health sessions for professionals and students who can't step away during the day.

Telehealth in California

Secure online mental health care, available anywhere in California — the same clinicians, from home.
