Evidence-Based Therapies

Therapeutic Modalities We Specialize In

CBT and DBT are the two most-researched, most-effective forms of psychotherapy in the world — and they're the backbone of our clinical work.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

The gold standard for anxiety, depression, PTSD, OCD, insomnia, and addiction. CBT identifies and changes the unhelpful thoughts and behaviors that drive emotional suffering — and gives you skills you'll use for life.

Explore CBT

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

The gold standard for borderline personality disorder, self-harm, emotion dysregulation, and complex addiction. DBT teaches concrete skills in mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness.

Explore DBT
Our Approach

Evidence-Based, Individualized, and Compassionate

Every person who walks through our doors has a unique story — a unique combination of genetics, history, trauma, and circumstances that shaped their relationship with substances or mental health challenges. Our treatment plans reflect that reality.

We use therapies supported by decades of research: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, EMDR, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Motivational Interviewing, and Medication-Assisted Treatment — customized to each individual, not applied uniformly.
