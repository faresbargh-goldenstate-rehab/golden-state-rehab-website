Our Approach

Healing requires both science and humanity

We believe in meeting people with respect, treating addiction and mental health as interconnected challenges that deserve integrated care.

Evidence-Based Care

Every treatment approach we use is grounded in research and clinical best practices. We combine the latest evidence with genuine compassion.

Integrated Treatment

We address mental health and addiction together, recognizing that these challenges are deeply interconnected and require unified care.

Flexible Scheduling

Our programs are designed around your life — not the other way around. From morning PHP to evening IOP, we fit recovery into your schedule.

Our Distinction

We stand apart through depth and dedication

Golden State Rehab is DHCS-licensed by the State of California (License #191643AP) and LegitScript certified, meeting the leading national standards for behavioral health care.

Our clinical team brings decades of combined experience in addiction medicine and psychiatric care, with therapists who specialize in trauma-informed treatment. We are committed to offering integrated programs that address both mental health and substance use together — an approach many facilities still treat separately.

  • Trauma-informed clinical approach
  • Dual diagnosis treatment specialists
  • Flexible outpatient scheduling
  • Dedicated alumni support network
  • Psychiatric care integrated with treatment
