Areas We Serve

Serving the Greater West Los Angeles Area

Our Westwood location puts evidence-based outpatient care within easy reach of the Westside. We welcome clients from across Los Angeles County — and offer telehealth throughout California.

Westwood

Our home neighborhood. Walk-in convenient for UCLA-area residents, students, and professionals seeking discreet, high-quality outpatient treatment.

Santa Monica

A short drive west. We serve Santa Monica residents needing flexible PHP and IOP scheduling that works around the beachside work-life balance.

Beverly Hills

Confidential, premium outpatient care for Beverly Hills clients who value privacy, discretion, and an elevated standard of clinical attention.

Brentwood

Minutes from Brentwood, we provide individual therapy, dual-diagnosis care, and family support for one of LA's most close-knit communities.

Culver City

Easy access from Culver City for working professionals — our evening IOP tracks let you keep your job and routine while you recover.

Greater Los Angeles

From the San Fernando Valley to the South Bay, we serve clients across LA County, with telehealth available anywhere in California.
