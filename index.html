Why Golden State Rehab

Evidence-based care and a plan built around your real life, at a private outpatient center in West Los Angeles.

Personalized treatment plans

Your care should fit you, not a template. You start with a full clinical assessment, and from there we shape a plan around your diagnosis, your goals, and your day-to-day life.

Licensed & certified

Licensed by California's DHCS (License #191643AP) and LegitScript certified. These are the standards that hold behavioral health to account.

Most insurance accepted

We work with most major PPO plans, including Aetna, Anthem, Cigna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, and United Healthcare. Checking your benefits is free and takes about a minute.

Admissions 24/7

A crisis rarely waits for business hours. You can reach our intake team by phone day or night, and we offer same-day assessments whenever possible.

Master's-level clinicians

You work directly with licensed therapists, psychiatrists, and certified addiction specialists, never interns. Their care draws on proven methods like CBT, DBT, EMDR, and medication-assisted treatment.

Flexible outpatient programs

PHP, IOP, telehealth, and individual therapy, all built to fit around work, family, and daily life. You stay in your own home and community while you heal.

Who we are

Treatment that meets you where you are

Golden State Rehab is a licensed addiction and mental health treatment center in West Los Angeles. We provide evidence-based outpatient care for adults across the greater LA area, including PHP, IOP, telehealth, and individual therapy.

We treat the whole person, not just a diagnosis. Our goal is simple: help you rebuild a life worth living, through individualized care grounded in respect, honesty, and clinical skill.

Process

Your path to treatment

Starting treatment is simpler than most people expect. We handle the logistics so you can focus on the work that matters.

01

First Contact

You call us or send in the intake form. We respond within hours to answer your questions and set up your assessment.

02

Clinical Assessment

You sit down with an intake specialist for a clinical interview. We listen to your history, get clear on what you need, and go through your options together.

03

Insurance Verification

We check your insurance coverage at no cost and explain exactly what your plan covers before you commit to anything.

04

Your First Session

You meet your therapist, join your first group, and begin the real work alongside people who understand what you're facing.

05

Treatment Momentum

You settle into your program, build trust with the people around you, and start gaining skills that hold up in daily life. This is where progress starts to feel real.

06

Continued Care

Recovery doesn't end at discharge. We stay with you through the transitions that follow and keep cheering you on as part of our alumni community.
