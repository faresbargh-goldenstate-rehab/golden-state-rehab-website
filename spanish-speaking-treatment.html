Care That Speaks Your Language

Treatment in Spanish — by People Who Truly Understand

For many people, the hardest part of asking for help is finding someone who understands not just your words, but your culture, your family, and your story. At Golden State Rehab, Spanish isn't an afterthought or a phone interpreter on speaker — it's a full treatment track delivered by Spanish-speaking clinicians and staff in West Los Angeles.

From the first phone call to therapy, family sessions, and aftercare, you and your loved ones can move through the entire process in Spanish. Recovery is hard enough — the language shouldn't be one more barrier.

Why It Matters

Why Bilingual Care Changes Outcomes

Mental health and recovery work happens through language — naming feelings, telling your story, rebuilding trust. When that happens in your first language, treatment goes deeper and families stay involved.

  • Therapy in your own words — nuance and emotion aren't lost in translation
  • Spanish-speaking staff from the first call through aftercare
  • Family sessions in Spanish, so loved ones are part of recovery
  • Culturally respectful care that honors your background and values
  • Clear, Spanish-language explanations of insurance, treatment, and next steps
