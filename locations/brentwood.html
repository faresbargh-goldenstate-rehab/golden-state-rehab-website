Outpatient Treatment Near Brentwood

Care for the Whole Family, Ten Minutes from Brentwood

Brentwood is a close-knit place — people know each other from the farmers market, the school pickup line, the coral trees on San Vicente. When addiction or a mental health condition reaches a household here, it rarely affects just one person, and families often come to us together. Golden State Rehab is a DHCS-licensed outpatient center at 1964 Westwood Blvd, and our approach is simple: treat the person, support the family. Our families page shows what that looks like.

The logistics work, too. Ten minutes down Wilshire or San Vicente puts you at our door, so a parent can drop the kids at school and still make a 9 a.m. group. And we're straightforward about what we are: an outpatient center offering PHP, IOP, and telehealth — no residential beds, no on-site detox. If medically supervised withdrawal has to come first, we refer you to a licensed detox facility and hold your place here.

Levels of Care

Outpatient Programs for Brentwood Residents

Partial Hospitalization (PHP)

Full treatment days, five days a week — our highest level of outpatient structure, with evenings back home in Brentwood.

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

Morning and evening tracks of several hours each, built to fit around work meetings and school-run schedules.

Online & Telehealth

Secure video therapy and psychiatry anywhere in California, for the weeks when the drive down Wilshire isn't possible.

Individual Therapy

Regular one-on-one sessions with your own therapist, drawing on CBT, DBT, and trauma-informed methods.

Mental Health Track

Care for depression, anxiety, PTSD, and bipolar disorder as primary conditions — a substance use diagnosis isn't required.

Spanish-Speaking Track

Complete programming delivered in Spanish by bilingual clinicians. Se habla español.

Local Support

Recovery Resources for Brentwood Families

The Westside recovery community isn't only for the person in treatment. Al-Anon meetings for family members are widely available alongside twelve-step meetings across Brentwood, Santa Monica, and West LA, so spouses and parents can find their own support too. Before discharge, we help every client map out a realistic weekly meeting plan.

When home isn't the right environment for early recovery, we coordinate referrals to structured sober living homes across Southern California, including Comeback Sober Living. Pairing a substance-free residence with our PHP or IOP gives daily structure without residential treatment.

Two numbers worth saving: SAMHSA's confidential helpline, 1-800-662-4357, answers around the clock in English and Spanish, and FindTreatment.gov lists licensed providers nationwide. In a mental health crisis, call or text 988.

Finishing a program here isn't goodbye — our alumni program keeps former clients connected to peers and staff long after the last session.
