Outpatient Treatment Near Mar Vista

Recovery That Fits a Mar Vista Routine

Mar Vista is a neighborhood of young families and long-time residents — people who build their lives close to home. Golden State Rehab fits that pattern. Our DHCS-licensed outpatient center at 1964 Westwood Blvd may be the closest full outpatient program to your door, often under ten minutes up Overland or Sawtelle. That proximity matters: when treatment slots in between school runs and work weeks instead of replacing them, staying with it becomes far more realistic.

We're just as direct about what we are not. This is an outpatient center with no beds and no on-site detox — you attend PHP, IOP, or telehealth sessions, then sleep at home. If medically supervised withdrawal has to come first, we say so plainly, connect you with a licensed detox facility, and hold your place here so treatment starts the moment detox ends.

Local Support

Recovery Resources Near Mar Vista

You won't have to drive far to find recovery community. Twelve-step and other peer support groups meet daily within a short drive of Mar Vista — in the neighborhood itself, in Culver City, and across Santa Monica. Before discharge, your therapist helps you build a weekly meeting plan you can actually keep.

If a substance-free home would strengthen your early recovery, we coordinate sober living referrals across Southern California, including operators like Comeback Sober Living. Pairing structured housing with our PHP or IOP gives your days a shape that supports the work you do in treatment.

Save two national resources as well: SAMHSA's free, confidential helpline at 1-800-662-4357 (24/7, English and Spanish) and the licensed-provider locator at FindTreatment.gov. In a mental health crisis, call or text 988 anytime.

Finishing treatment doesn't mean losing your people, either — our alumni program keeps you connected with staff and peers well beyond your last session.
