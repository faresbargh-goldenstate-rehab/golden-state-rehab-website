Outpatient Treatment Near WeHo

Recovery Is Already Part of West Hollywood Life

West Hollywood has one of the most active recovery communities in the country. Getting sober here is normal — meetings run all day, and nobody blinks when you order a soda. Golden State Rehab backs that culture up with DHCS-licensed outpatient treatment about 25 minutes west, straight down Santa Monica Blvd. We treat addiction and mental health together, so if depression or anxiety sits underneath the substance use, our dual diagnosis care addresses both at once.

We also know WeHo doesn't run on a nine-to-five clock. Bartenders, performers, stylists, and service-industry workers need care that fits around late shifts, so we offer daytime and evening sessions plus telehealth. Every client can count on an affirming, judgment-free environment — LGBTQ+ clients are welcomed and respected exactly as they are. One honest note: we're an outpatient center, not a detox. If you need medical detox first, we refer you to a licensed facility and hold your place until you're ready.

Local Support

Recovery Resources Around West Hollywood

West Hollywood's meeting schedule is famously dense. Twelve-step meetings and secular alternatives run morning, noon, and night across the city, and many LGBTQ+-focused meetings exist in the community. Whatever your path, our team helps you build a weekly support plan before you finish treatment.

If a substance-free home would help, we coordinate sober living referrals across Southern California, including homes like Comeback Sober Living. Pairing structured housing with our PHP or IOP program gives early recovery a stable base.

Two national resources worth saving: SAMHSA's free, confidential helpline at 1-800-662-4357 (24/7, English and Spanish) and the treatment locator at FindTreatment.gov. In a mental health crisis, call or text 988.

And when treatment ends, the community doesn't — our alumni program keeps you connected to peers and staff long after your last session.
