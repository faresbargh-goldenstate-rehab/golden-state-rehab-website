Outpatient Treatment Near Santa Monica

Structured Care, About 15 Minutes from Santa Monica

If you live in Santa Monica, getting serious help for addiction or a mental health condition doesn't have to mean leaving the Westside. Golden State Rehab sits at 1964 Westwood Blvd — a straight shot east on Olympic, roughly 15 minutes from downtown Santa Monica. You get full days or focused evenings of physician-led, DHCS-licensed treatment, then go home to sleep in your own bed. Many of our clients keep their jobs in the tech offices, hotels, restaurants, and healthcare settings along the coast the whole time they're with us.

One thing we'll always be straight about: we're an outpatient center. There are no beds here and no detox on site. If withdrawal management needs to happen first, we connect you with a licensed detox partner and hold your spot, so treatment begins the moment you're medically ready.

Local Support

Recovery Support In and Around Santa Monica

Santa Monica has one of the densest recovery-meeting scenes in Southern California. Twelve-step groups and other peer-support meetings run every day of the week across the city — early mornings near the beach, evenings in church halls and community rooms — so building a support routine close to home is realistic. Before you finish treatment, we help you map out a weekly meeting plan you'll actually keep.

If home isn't a stable place to recover right now, we refer clients to structured sober living homes across Southern California, including Comeback Sober Living. Pairing a substance-free household with our PHP or IOP schedule gives early recovery a much firmer footing.

Keep two national resources handy as well: SAMHSA's free, confidential helpline at 1-800-662-4357 answers 24/7 in English and Spanish, and FindTreatment.gov lists licensed providers nationwide. If you're in a mental health crisis, call or text 988 any time.

And finishing treatment isn't goodbye. Our alumni program keeps Santa Monica clients connected to peers and staff long after the last session.

Nearby communities we serve: If you're closer to another part of the Westside, we also provide outpatient treatment near Venice, Brentwood, and Pacific Palisades — same team, same building, whichever neighborhood you're coming from.
