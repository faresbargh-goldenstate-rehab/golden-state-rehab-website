Our Story

Began

A community that needed better care. A team that refused to accept the status quo.

The Beginning

Born from a gap in care — and a refusal to ignore it

In 2026, our founders recognized something troubling: Los Angeles had no shortage of treatment centers, yet countless people were falling through the cracks. Facilities treated addiction or mental health — rarely both. Programs were rigid, impersonal, and designed around the institution rather than the person.

The founding team had seen this firsthand — in colleagues, in family members, in clients who cycled in and out of care without ever experiencing real healing. They believed the problem wasn't the people struggling; it was the fragmented system meant to help them.

Golden State Rehab was built to do something different: treat the whole person, with integrated care that addresses addiction and mental health together, in a setting that feels like healing — not a hospital.

Our Purpose

We believe healing is possible — for everyone

"Our mission is to help people reclaim their lives by offering thoughtful, individualized treatment rooted in respect, honesty, and clinical expertise. We walk beside every client through healing, growth, and long-term recovery — ensuring no one has to navigate this journey alone."

We exist for the person who has been told they can't get better. For the family that doesn't know where to turn. For anyone who deserves a fresh start and a team that will genuinely fight for their recovery.

What We Stand For

Our core values guide every decision we make

From the first phone call to the last day of treatment — and beyond — these values shape how we show up for every person in our care.

Integrity

We operate with radical honesty — with our clients, their families, and ourselves. No overpromising. No shortcuts. Just a genuine commitment to doing right by the people who trust us.

Compassion

Every person who walks through our doors deserves to be treated with dignity and warmth. We meet clients where they are — never where we think they should be.

Clinical Excellence

Evidence-based, trauma-informed care delivered by specialists who stay current with the latest research. Our clinical standards are never compromised — no matter what.
